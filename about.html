Our Technology

At HydroLife, we leverage cutting-edge technology to address global water challenges. Our solutions are designed for efficiency, sustainability, and impact.

Compact Purification System

A lightweight, portable system for individual use, capable of filtering up to 10 liters of water per day.

Family-Sized Filter Kit

A larger kit designed for families or small communities, filtering up to 50 liters daily.

Agricultural Desalination System

A solar-powered pod capable of producing up to 1,000 liters of fresh water per day from seawater or brackish water.

Community Desalination Unit

A larger system designed for agricultural use, providing up to 5,000 liters of desalinated water daily.

Household Water Monitor

An IoT device that monitors household water usage in real-time, providing insights and alerts to reduce waste.

Agricultural Irrigation Tracker

A system that tracks water usage in irrigation systems, optimizing water distribution and reducing consumption.
